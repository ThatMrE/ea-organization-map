## Open Philanthropy (Moskovitz / Tuna)

**Founded:** 2017  ·  **Scale:** ~$400M+/year  ·  **Recipients in map:** 44

**Where the money comes from**

- **Dustin Moskovitz** (Co-funder) — _Co-founder of Facebook & Asana_
- **Cari Tuna** (Co-funder) — _President of Good Ventures_

**Who decides what to fund**

- **Alexander Berger** — Co-CEO (Global Catastrophic Risks)
- **Emily Oehlsen** — Co-CEO (Global Health & Wellbeing)

> Vehicle for Moskovitz/Tuna giving via Good Ventures. Biggest single funder in the EA ecosystem.

_Source: <https://www.openphilanthropy.org/about/>_

---

## SFF (Tallinn-backed)

**Founded:** 2019  ·  **Scale:** ~$20–40M/year  ·  **Recipients in map:** 19

- **Jaan Tallinn** (Primary backer) — _Skype & Kazaa co-founder_
- **Jed McCaleb** (Backer) — _Stellar & eDonkey co-founder_

**Who decides what to fund**

- **Andrew Critch** — Founder of S-process / SFF format
